MIDNIGHT MARAUDER - MURDER ON THE SKY EMPRESS

The Story... so far: In the heart of the roaring 20s, a group of diverse individuals finds themselves on a late-night journey on the luxurious transatlantic zeppelin, The Sky Empress. As they soar over the Atlantic, the renowned millionaire, art collector, and adventurer Leonard Van Dyke, is found dead in his private cabin, and a priceless painting missing. Among the passengers, the detective Frederick Blythe and his enigmatic assistant takes it upon themselves to investigate. Suspects include a disgruntled artist, a wealthy heiress, a mysterious foreign diplomat, Van Dyke’s estranged son, and the zeppelin’s enigmatic captain. One by one, Blythe interviews each suspect and discovers each had a reason to wish Van Dyke harm. As the plot thickens, Blythe finds a cryptic note in Van Dyke’s cabin reading, “Art is an illusion. So am I.”

Will our intrepid detective and his enigmatic assistant unravel the intricate web of this mystery, leaving no stone unturned? Or will this enigma become an enduring tale of the one that got away, forever etched in the annals of Blythe's untold stories? Only time will reveal the truth, as they delve deeper into the shadows to uncover the secrets that lie within.
